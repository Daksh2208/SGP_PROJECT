Anthurium Cultivation Guide

Anthuriums are tropical plants known for their vibrant, heart-shaped flowers and glossy leaves. They're popular as ornamental plants and require specific care to thrive.

Optimal Growing Conditions for Anthurium

Climate

  • Temperature: Anthuriums prefer temperatures between 21째C to 29째C (70째F to 85째F).
  • Light: Bright, indirect light is ideal. Avoid direct sunlight, which can scorch the leaves.

Season

  • Planting Season: Best planted in spring or early summer.
  • Flowering Season: Anthuriums can bloom year-round with the right conditions.

Soil

  • Type: Well-drained, coarse soil with organic matter like peat or pine bark.
  • pH Level: Slightly acidic soil with a pH range of 5.5 to 6.5 is ideal.

Planting and Care

Planting

  • Planting Depth: Plant the root ball at the same level it was growing in its previous container.

Watering

  • Frequency: Water when the top inch of soil feels dry, ensuring proper drainage.
