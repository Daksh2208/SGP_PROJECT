Rob Farming Guide

1. What is Rob?

Rob refers to a type of shrub or plant, typically known for its aromatic leaves and culinary uses. Commonly used in herbal teas and cooking, Rob adds flavor and nutrition to various dishes.

2. Characteristics of Rob

  • Appearance: Rob plants are bushy with green leaves, often growing 1-3 feet tall.
  • Flavor: The leaves have a strong aromatic flavor, often used in cooking and herbal remedies.
  • Uses: Primarily used in teas, seasonings, and traditional medicines.

5. Harvesting Rob

Harvest leaves as needed, cutting them off without damaging the plant. Best harvested in the morning when the essential oils are most concentrated.
