Urad (Black Gram) Farming Guide

1. Seasons for Growing Urad (Black Gram)

Urad, also known as black gram, is a warm-season legume. The best seasons for growing urad are:

  • Kharif Season: Urad is typically planted with the onset of the monsoon (June to July).
  • Rabi Season: In irrigated areas, urad can also be grown as a rabi crop during November to December.
